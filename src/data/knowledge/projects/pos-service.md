# POS Service Management System

## Overview

The POS Service Management System is a web application developed at Millennium IMS Pvt Ltd to manage service operations for retail businesses. It streamlines service requests, repairs, customer interactions, billing, and service tracking through a centralized platform.

Mohanraj Murugesan contributed as a Full Stack Software Engineer, building customer-facing interfaces, backend services, and REST APIs while maintaining production features.
