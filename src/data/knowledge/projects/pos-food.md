# Food Point of Sale (POS) System

## Overview

The Food Point of Sale (POS) System is a restaurant and food service management application developed at Millennium IMS Pvt Ltd. It helps restaurants and food businesses manage orders, billing, kitchen operations, menu management, and customer service through a centralized platform.

Mohanraj Murugesan contributed as a Full Stack Software Engineer, developing frontend applications, backend services, and REST APIs to support restaurant operations and improve order processing efficiency.
