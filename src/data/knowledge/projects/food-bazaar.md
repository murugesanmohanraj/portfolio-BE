# Food Bazaar E-commerce Platform

## Overview

The Food Bazaar E-commerce Platform is an online shopping application developed at Millennium IMS Pvt Ltd. It enables customers to browse products, manage shopping carts, place orders, and complete purchases through a responsive web interface.

Mohanraj Murugesan contributed as a Full Stack Software Engineer, building frontend features, backend services, and REST APIs to support the customer shopping experience.
