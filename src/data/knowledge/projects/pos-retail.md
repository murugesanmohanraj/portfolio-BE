# Retail Point of Sale (POS) System

## Overview

The Retail Point of Sale (POS) System is an enterprise retail management application developed at Millennium IMS Pvt Ltd. It enables retail businesses to manage billing, inventory, product catalogs, customers, payments, promotions, and multiple store locations from a centralized platform.

Mohanraj Murugesan contributed as a Full Stack Software Engineer, developing customer-facing web applications, backend APIs, and business modules used in daily retail operations.
