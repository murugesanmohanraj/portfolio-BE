# GlobalLogic

## Company Overview

GlobalLogic is a digital engineering company that helps organizations build digital products, platforms, and intelligent experiences. It is part of the Hitachi Group and works with clients across industries including healthcare, automotive, sustainability, finance, telecommunications, and manufacturing.

Mohanraj joined GlobalLogic in June 2024 as a Senior Full Stack Software Engineer and currently works on the Wood Mackenzie Lens Platform.

### Wood Mackenzie

Wood Mackenzie is a global provider of data, analytics, and insights for the energy and natural resources industries.

The Lens Platform helps organizations analyze energy markets, carbon emissions, sustainability initiatives, supply chains, and investment opportunities using large datasets and interactive dashboards.

Mohanraj works on customer-facing applications that enable users to explore sustainability data efficiently.
